Definition

A mechanism that computes weighted sums over values, where weights are derived from query-key dot products, enabling dynamic context-sensitive representations.
Why do we use scaled dot product?
Scaling by √d_k prevents the dot products from growing large in magnitude, which would push softmax into regions with tiny gradients.
